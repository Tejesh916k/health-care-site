Age Categories

Age for 18 - 30

Proteins: Lean meats, eggs, tofu, beans, and lentils for muscle growth and repair.

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily.

Calcium & Vitamin D (Milk, yogurt, cheese, leafy greens) for strong bones.

Age for 30 - 50

Include whole grains, nuts, and fiber-rich foods for a healthy heart.

Regular strength training and cardio exercises.

Vitamin C & Omega-3 for better immunity and brain function.

Age for 50+

Low-impact exercises like yoga, walking, and swimming.

Increase calcium intake for bone strength.

Hydration & balanced diet with antioxidants for healthy aging.
